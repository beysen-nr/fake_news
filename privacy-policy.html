Privacy Policy

Last updated:14 December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the application Fake News Detection (“the App”) handles user data.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Users can analyze text content without creating an account or providing personal details.

2. Text Analysis

Any text entered by the user is sent temporarily to a backend server solely for the purpose of analysis. The text is not stored, logged, or shared with third parties after the analysis is completed.

3. Data Storage

The App does not permanently store user-submitted content, analysis results, or usage data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses a remote server to process text analysis requests. No personal data is transmitted or retained beyond what is necessary to perform the requested analysis.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies, analytics, tracking technologies, or advertising services.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
